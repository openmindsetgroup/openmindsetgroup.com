All information you enter in Petaly is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have servers, cloud storage, or any way to access your data. Your entries, activities, and settings never leave your phone.

We don't require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use Petaly. There's nothing to hack because we don't store anything.

Your data is encrypted on your device using industry-standard SQLCipher encryption, protecting it even if someone gains physical access to your device.

You can export your data at any time. Exports are saved locally and you control what happens with them.

Data Deletion

To delete all your Petaly data, simply uninstall the app. Since everything is stored locally, uninstalling removes all traces of your data.
